About PraxisVerify

The Expert-in-the-Loop Platform for Auditable Compliance

Our Mission

PraxisVerify is building the secure, compliant bridge between life sciences companies and the certified human expertise they need. In an industry where AI can draft documents faster than ever, regulators still require human expert verification. We provide that critical link — faster, safer, and fully audit-ready.

The current process for engaging regulatory experts is broken. Companies spend months on vendor qualification, QMS onboarding, and contractual negotiations before a single document gets reviewed. PraxisVerify replaces this with one-time platform credentialing, reducing expert engagement from 6 months to 48 hours.

The Founder

Lisa Donlon is the Founder and Director of PraxisVerify. A quality and regulatory professional, Lisa watched companies struggle with slow, expensive expert engagement for document verification across the MedTech and Pharma industries. She founded PraxisVerify to solve this problem with a purpose-built platform that makes expert verification audit-ready, secure, and on-demand.

Lisa holds a background in quality management systems and regulatory compliance, with hands-on experience navigating ISO 13485, 21 CFR Part 11, and EU MDR requirements.

What We Do

PraxisVerify is a secure marketplace connecting life sciences companies to pre-vetted regulatory experts. The platform provides:

  • Zero-trust document review — experts review in-browser only. No downloads, no email, no risk. Automatic 90-day deletion.
  • Pre-vetted experts — regulatory professionals with verified credentials in ISO 13485, 21 CFR Part 11, EU MDR, and FDA submissions.
  • Complete audit trails — every action logged and traceable, meeting 21 CFR Part 11 requirements.
  • Rapid onboarding — one-time platform credentialing replaces per-client vendor qualification.

Who We Serve

PraxisVerify serves MedTech manufacturers, pharmaceutical companies, and medical device companies that need expert verification of regulatory documentation. Typical use cases include:

  • FDA 510(k) submission review
  • EU MDR Clinical Evaluation Report (CER) validation
  • ISO 13485 gap analysis
  • Pharma batch record review
  • Design History File (DHF) verification
