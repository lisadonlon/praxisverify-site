What We're Building

PraxisVerify is a secure platform that connects life sciences companies to pre-vetted regulatory experts. Upload documents to a zero-trust environment. Experts review in-browser — no downloads, no email, no risk.

  • Speed

    Pre-vetted experts ready in hours, not the 6+ months traditional onboarding requires. One-time platform credentialing replaces per-client vendor qualification.

  • Security

    Zero-trust architecture with in-browser review only. No document downloads. Automatic 90-day deletion. Your IP never leaves the platform.

  • Compliance

    Complete audit trails meeting 21 CFR Part 11 requirements. Structured findings with expert credentials. Every action logged and traceable.

  • Expert-in-the-Loop

    AI can draft your documents faster than ever. But regulators require human expert verification. We provide the experts — pre-vetted, QMS-trained, and ready to work.
